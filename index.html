Welcome to the Zodiac Card Collection

Explore the 12 zodiac signs and their unique characteristics

Aries (March 21 - April 19)

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac and represents the fire element. People born under this sign are known for their courage, leadership and determination. They are adventurous and energetic.

Taurus (April 20 - May 20)

Taurus is an earth sign known for its stability and practicality. People born under this sign are often reliable, patient and determined. They appreciate beauty and comfort.

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)

Gemini is an air sign known for its versatility and curiosity. People born under this sign are often adaptable, communicative and witty. They have a love for learning and exploring.

Cancer (June 21 - July 22)

Cancer is a water sign known for its emotional depth and nurturing nature. People born under this sign are often compassionate, intuitive and protective. They value family and home life.

Leo (July 23 - August 22)

Leo is a fire sign known for its charisma and leadership qualities. People born under this sign are often confident, generous and enthusiastic.They enjoy the spotlight.

Virgo (August 23 - September 22)

Virgo is an earth sign known for its attention to detail and practicality. People born under this sign are often analytical, organized and helpful. They strive for perfection.

Libra (September 23 - October 22)

Libra is an air sign known for its balance and charm. People born under this sign are often diplomatic, sociable and fair-minded. They seek harmony in relationships.

Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)

Scorpio is a water sign known for its intensity and passion. People born under this sign are often resourceful, determined and perceptive. They value deep connections.

Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)

Sagittarius is a fire sign known for its curiosity and optimism. People born under this sign are often enthusiastic, sociable and open-minded. They seek knowledge and freedom.

Capricorn (December 22 - January 19)

Capricorn is an earth sign known for its discipline and ambition. People born under this sign are often responsible, patient, and practical. They value achievement and stability.

Aquarius (January 20 - February 18)

Aquarius is an air sign known for its innovation and independence. People born under this sign are often open-minded, humanitarian, and unconventional. They value progress and individuality.

Pisces (February 19 - March 20)

Pisces is a water sign known for its sensitivity and creativity. People born under this sign are often compassionate, artistic, and empathetic. They value emotional connections.
